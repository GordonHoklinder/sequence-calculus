Short introduction to the calculus of sequences

This short book tries to cover the basics of the calculus of sequences as I believe this intriguing topic remains underapriciated, especially compared to its continuous counterpart. I myself am not an expert in this area, therefore the book tries to be rather shallow and focuses on intuitive explanation of the topics.
